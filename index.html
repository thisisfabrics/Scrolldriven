3D Applier

We announce a design contestfrom 108

And propose you to participate in developing our new merch

Technical task

You are provided with a couple of template 3D models of our merch things without textures

Also you will have special image hints and w/b masks that will your guides. Using that, you need to create textures and apply them to our models.

Here we provide resources you might use.
You can get more information on how to draw on the 3D Applier page, as well as test your textures.

Download the hint
Download the mask
Download the model

And here you can find logotypes and other detalization

After you are done, fill this Google form.
Thank you for participation!

Rules

    • You are going to draw a square texture for our templates of supposed set of merch, 8K resolution is not necessary, work in a convenient format
      Do not change unwrapping of the models
      3D Applier is a web interface, using which you can interact with our 3d models with the mouse, change their textures and get hints, but if you want, of course, you are able to use other services
    • Casual and light style is welcome, but work in heavy raster if you want!
      You have no restrictions on changing our symbolics or creation new design patterns
      Do not use Innopolis property, brands, and trademarks. Or do it very carefully, stilization is better
      Your submitted work has to be compatible with Creative Commons 0
      Keep source files until the end of the contest
      The winner will be chosen based on the opinion of one-zero-eight and, maybe, yours, follow us @one-zero-eight
    • Prize

    You can become the owner of this slay lantern with stars
